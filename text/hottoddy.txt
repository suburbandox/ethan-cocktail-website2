The Hot Toddy is just the thing to warm you up on a chilly winter evening. it is basically a warm Old-Fashioned.

## Ingredients

- 2 lemon wedges
- 1 1/2 oz bourbon
- 3/4 oz honey syrup (3:1 honey:water)
- 1 dash Angostura bitters
- 4 oz boiling water
- Garnish: lemon wedge +/- cinnamon stick or grated nutmeg

## Preparation

1. Squeeze lemon wedges into mug, then add bourbon, honey syrup, and bitters
2. Pour in boiling water, and stir.
3. Garnish with lemon wedge, and cinnamon or nutmeg
