Papa Hemingway liked hanging out in Cuba. He fished, he drank, he daillied, and he wrote. El Floridita bar in Havana was his favorite tippling spot, and there is a bronze statue of the author at the bar today. This version of the daiquiri is reputed to have been his favorite.

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 oz white rum
- 1/2 oz Luxardo maraschino liqueur
- 1 oz fresh grapefruit juice
- 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
- 1-2 tsp simple syrup
- Garnish: lime wedge

## Preparation

1. Fill a mixing glass or shaker with ice
2. Add the rum, lime juice, grapefruit juice, Luxardo liqueur and simple syrup
3. Shake vigorously until well-chilled
4. Strain into chilled coupe
5. Garnish with lime wedge

## Comments
- "Write drunk, edit sober" - Earnest Hemingway
