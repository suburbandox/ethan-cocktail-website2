The Paper Plane reminds me of pre-flight cocktails in the Polaris Lounge. Feel free to experiment with other amari, italian bitter-sweet liqueurs. If you substitute Camapri for Aperol, it will be more bitter.

## Ingredients

- 3/4 oz bourbon
- 3/4 oz Aperol
- 3/4 oz Amaro Nonino
- 3/4 oz fresh lime juice

## Preparation

1. Fill a shaker with ice
2. Add the bourbon, Aperol, amaro, and lime juice
3. Shake vigorously until well-chilled
4. Strain into chilled coupe or Nick and Nora glass
