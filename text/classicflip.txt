The Classic Flip is rich and not too alcoholic, with its base being fortified wine such as cream or oloroso sherry. Feel free to try with other bases such as tawny port, rum, or other spirits

## Ingredients

- 2 oz sherry or spirit of your choice
- 2 tsp sugar
- 1 whole egg
- Garnish: nutmeg

## Preparation

1. Add all ingredients except the nutmeg to a shaker
2. Shake vigorously for one minute
3. Fill shaker with ice and shake again, for a minute
4. Double strain into a chilled coupe
5. Garnish with a dusting of nutmeg
