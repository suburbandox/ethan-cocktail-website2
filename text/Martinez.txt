The Martinez is basically a martini made with sweet rather than dry vermouth. It is a smooth and slightly sweeter alternative for someone who is learning to love gin.

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 oz gin
- 1 1/2 oz sweet vermouth
- 1 tsp Luxardo Marischino liqueur
- 2 dashes orange bitters
- Garnish: lemon twist

## Preparation

1. Fill a shaker or mixing glass with ice
2. Add the gin, sweet vermouth, Luxardo, and orange bitters
3. Stir vigorously until well-chilled
4. Pour into chilled (fill with ice and let sit) martini, coupe, or Nick and Nora glass
5. Garnish with twist
