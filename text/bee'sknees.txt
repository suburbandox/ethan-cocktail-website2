The Bee's Knees uses lemon juice as a souring agent. Lemon juice is less sharp a flavor than lime juice, which would overwhelm the honey. Different honeys will give different flavor profiles.

## Ingredients

- 2 oz gin
- 3/4 oz lemon juice
- 3/4 oz honey syrup [see below]
- Garnish: none

## Preparation

1. Honey Syrup: Mix 3 parts honey with 1 part water, whisk until blended. Refrigerate until used.
2. Fill a mixing glass or shaker with ice
3. Add the gin, lemon juice, and honey syrup
4. Shake vigorously until well-chilled
5. Strain into coupe
