The classic Sidecar is made with cognac, curacao liqueur, and lemon juice. Feel free to substitute: brandy for cognac, triple sec for curacao. Please use only fresh lemon juice! Imagine the fresh air on your face as you're riding down the boulevard in your motorcycle's side car.

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 oz cognac
- 3/4 oz fresh lemon juice
- 1 oz Cointreau or other orange liqueur
- Garnish: orange or lemon twist

## Preparation

1. Fill a shaker with ice
2. Add the cognac, lemon juice, and orange liqueur
3. Shake vigorously until well-chilled
4. Strain into chilled coupe
5. Garnish with twist
