The Classic Whisky Highball. Your father's drink. Scotch and soda. Why whisky and not whiskey? Because that's how you spell scotch. Always serve in a tall glass!

## Ingredients

- 2 oz scotch
- 6 oz cold seltzer or club soda
- Garnish: lemon wedge

## Preparation

1. Pour the scotch into a Highball glass
2. Add three ice cubes and stir for 3 second
3. Add the seltzer and stir once
4. Garnish with lemon wedgge
