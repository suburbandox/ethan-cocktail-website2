The Brown Derby was created in 1930, and it's named after a classic diner that was shaped like a hat. It tastes lighter than most whiskey cocktails, with the grapefruit juice , lemon juice, and honey syrup brightening the caramel notes from the bourbon.

## Ingredients

- 2 oz bourbon
- 1 oz grapefruit juice
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 1/2 oz honey syrup
- Garnish: none

## Preparation

1. Honey Syrup: Mix 3 parts honey with 1 part water, whisk until blended. Refrigerate until used.
2. Fill a mixing glass or shaker with ice
3. Add the bourbon, juices, and honey syrup
4. Shake vigorously until well-chilled
5. Strain into chilled coupe glass
