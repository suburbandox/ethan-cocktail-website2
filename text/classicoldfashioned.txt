The Classic Old Fashioned is a balance between the sharpness of the alcohol, the sweetness of sugar, and a dash of bitters to make it more interesting to the palate.

## Ingredients

- 2 oz bourbon
- 1 sugar cube
- 2 dashes Angostura bitters
- Garnish: lemon and/or orange twist

## Preparation

1. Muddle the sugar cube and bitters in an Old-Fashioned glass
2. Add the bourbon and ice (1 large cube if available otherwise several normal sized cubes)
3. Stir until well-chilled
4. Garnish with twists
