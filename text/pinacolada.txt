The Pina Colada is the beach version of the Flip. Also related to a Sour, the coconut cream brings it into the Flip family. Ideally served with a straw and umbrella!

## Ingredients

- 2 oz white rum
- 1/2 oz dark or black rum
- 1 1/2 oz pineapple juice
- 1 1/2 oz coconut cream
- Garnish: umbrella, pineapple wedge, brandied cherry ...

## Preparation

1. Add all ingredients to a shaker
2. Add small handful of ice
3. Shake vigorously for one minute
4. Pour into double rocks glass or other suitable resting spot
5. Fill glass with crushed ice
6. Garnish with pineapple, cherry, umbrella, etc
