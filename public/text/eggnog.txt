The eggnog experience of most people derives from carboard containers in the dairy section, usually found during the winter holiday season. Those are typically overly sweet. This recipe is more restrained. Grown up eggnog.

## Ingredients

- 3/4 oz aged rum
- 3/4 oz cognac or brandy
- 1/4 tsp vanilla
- 3/4 oz simple syrup
- 1 oz heavy cream
- 1 whole egg
- Garnish: cinnamon and nutmeg

## Preparation

1. Add all ingredients except the cinnamon nutmeg to a shaker
2. "Dry" shake vigorously for one minute
3. Fill shaker with ice and shake again, for a minute
4. Double strain into a chilled old fashioned glass
5. Garnish with a dusting of cinnamon and nutmeg
