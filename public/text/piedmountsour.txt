## Ingredients

- 1/2 oz bourbon
- 3/4 oz barolo chinato cocchi
- 3/4 oz egg white
- 1/2 oz lemon juice
- 1/2 oz lem
- 1 tsp orgeat

## Preparation

1. Fill coupe with ice
2. Dry shake all ingredients, then shake again on ice
3. Strain into chilled glasses
4. Shake vigorously until well-chilled
5. Strain into chilled coupe glass

## Comments
- I only used 1/2 oz egg white since 1 large egg gave me 1 oz
