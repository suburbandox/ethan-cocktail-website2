The Classic Martini is the very essence of a spirit-forward adult beverage. This recipie may be viewed as a template that can be changed with the type/brand of liquor, the type/amount of vermouth, and any additional flavorings. Try it with a spray of absinthe or pernod!

## Ingredients

- 2 oz gin or vodka
- 1 oz dry vermouth
- 1 dash Angostura bitters
- Garnish: olive(s!) or lemon twist

## Preparation

1. Fill a shaker or mixing glass with ice
2. Add the gin or vodka, dry vermouth, and Angostura bitters
3. Stir vigorously until well-chilled
4. Pour into chilled (fill with ice and let sit) martini, coupe, or Nick and Nora glass
5. Garnish with Olive or twist (if you are feeling Dickensian)
