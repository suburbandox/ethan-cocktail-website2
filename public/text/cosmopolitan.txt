The Classic Old Fashioned is a balance between the sharpness of the alcohol, the sweetness of sugar, and a dash of bitters to make it more interesting to the palate.

## Ingredients

- 2 oz citrus vodka
- 3/4 oz orange liqueur
- 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
- 1 oz cranberry juice
- Garnish: lime wheel

## Preparation

1. Fill a shaker with ice
2. Add the vodka, orange liqueur, and juices
3. Shake vigorously until well-chilled
4. Strain into chilled coupe or cocktail glass
5. Garnish with a lime wheel
